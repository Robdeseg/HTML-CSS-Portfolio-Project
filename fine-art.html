FINE ART


At the Willem Platerink Photography Studio we endeavour to source only the most sustainable wood when it comes to our framing. We use obeche and ramin, each with their own specific qualities, and both “soft-hardwoods” which allows for a muscular finish while remaining practical for hanging purposes. We encourage clients towards a two-inch black frame with a hand-painted soft-silver in-line trim. We also recommend to pair with a four-inch mat. Of course, these are only suggestions, and should you have specific requirements we would be pleased to discuss them with you. All prices on application.
