FINE ART


At the Willem Platerink Photography Studio we endeavour to source and use only the most sustainable wood when it comes to our framing. We use obeche and ramin, each with their own specific qualities, and both “soft-hardwoods” which allows for a muscular finish while remaining practical for hanging purposes. We encourage clients towards a four-inch black frame with a hand-painted soft-gold in-line trim. We also recommend an equal width mat. Of course, these are only suggestions, and should you have any specific requirements we would be pleased to discuss them with you directly. All prices on application.
